What is the Museum

Not only is the museum a trailblazing cultural capital project for UCD, it also allows both partners to expand beyond their traditional physical spaces. Through collaborative programming and promotion, MoLI will act as a catalyst through which to explore deeper links between UCD and the National Library in terms of collections, research, education and networks ‐ all the while engaging the public with their exceptional collections and scholarship through a vibrant new visitor attraction. The museums development has also been made possible through significant capital support from Failte Ireland, Irelands tourism development authority.
